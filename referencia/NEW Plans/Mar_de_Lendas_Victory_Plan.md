# Plan: Mar de Lendas Victory

## 1. Core Concept
A world of endless oceans, mysterious islands, and legendary treasures. Players are pirates, marines, or adventurers with their own ship and crew, seeking fame, freedom, or the ultimate treasure. The tone is inspired by One Piece, focusing on grand adventure, unique powers, and the bonds of a crew.

## 2. Key Mechanics
- **Sea Fruits:** A new Advantage category. Eating one grants a powerful and bizarre Kit (e.g., Rubber-Man, Fire-Woman, Beast-Man) but comes with the "Curse of the Sea" (Weakness to being submerged in water).
- **Ship Rules:** The crew's ship is a shared character with its own stats (Hull, Speed, Cannons), advantages, and can be upgraded.
- **Ambition:** A new power system with 3 tiers (Observation, Armament, Conqueror), allowing for extraordinary feats beyond normal skills.

## 3. New Player Options
- **Kits:** Swordsman of the Seven Seas, Genius Navigator, Battle Cook, Sniper King, plus dozens of unique kits from "Sea Fruits".
- **Advantages:** Loyal Crew, Beloved Ship, Fame (or Infamy).

## 4. Setting Overview
The "Grand Route" is a treacherous sea where bizarre phenomena, powerful monsters, and the tyrannical World Marine rule. Four immensely powerful pirate lords, the Sea Emperors, control the final, most dangerous stretch of ocean.

## 5. Proposed Book Structure
1.  Introduction: The Age of Pirates
2.  Character Creation: Forging Your Legend
3.  Sea Fruits: Powers of the Ocean
4.  New Kits & Advantages
5.  Life at Sea: Ship Rules & Exploration
6.  The World of the Grand Route
7.  Running an Epic Saga
8.  Threats: Marines, Monsters & Rival Crews

# Plan: Criaturas Victory

## 1. Core Concept
A world where humans, "Trainers," form bonds with magical creatures to battle, explore, and compete. Players travel the world, challenging Arena Leaders, thwarting evil teams, and aiming to become the Champion. The focus is on the creatures as the primary combatants.

## 2. Key Mechanics
- **Creatures as Advanced Allies:** A complete overhaul of the Ally advantage. Creatures have their own stats, elemental types, special moves, and can evolve.
- **Type System:** A rock-paper-scissors system of elemental types that grants Advantage or Disadvantage on attacks.
- **Evolution:** Rules for creatures to evolve into more powerful forms when they meet certain conditions, changing their stats, type, and appearance.

## 3. New Player Options
- **Trainer Kits:** Type Specialist, Creature Breeder, Battle Coordinator, Ruin Explorer. These kits grant bonuses to the trainer's creatures.
- **Advantages:** Creature Sync, Capture Luck, Habitat Knowledge.

## 4. Setting Overview
The region of Aria is filled with diverse biomes, each housing different creatures. The Aria League governs the official battle circuit. The villainous Team Shadow seeks to exploit creatures for their own nefarious goals.

## 5. Proposed Book Structure
1.  Introduction: The World of Creature Trainers
2.  The Trainer: Character Creation
3.  New Trainer Kits & Advantages
4.  Creature Mechanics: Capturing, Training & Evolution
5.  The Creature Compendium (A "Pokedex" with ~50 base creatures)
6.  The Aria Region: Locations & Arenas
7.  Running a Journey Campaign
8.  Antagonists & Legendary Creatures

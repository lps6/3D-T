# Plan: Fronteira Estelar Victory

## 1. Core Concept
A grimdark future where vast galactic empires clash for dominance. Players are elite soldiers, agents, or free mercenaries caught in massive conflicts. The tone is inspired by Warhammer 40k and classic space opera, focusing on tactical combat and the sheer scale of interstellar war, adapted to the cinematic 3D&T Victory system.

## 2. Key Mechanics
- **Squad Rules:** Mechanics for players to control small units of soldiers, giving orders, and managing squad resources.
- **Battle Scale:** Expanding the Ningen-Sugoi-Kiodai-Kami scale to represent vehicles, artillery, and massive war machines. A tank might be Sugoi, a walker Kiodai, and a battleship Kami.
- **Vehicle Rules:** Simple rules for piloting and fighting with vehicles, from jeeps and tanks to starfighters.

## 3. New Player Options
- **Kits:** Imperial Marine, Psychic Warrior, Infiltrator Agent, Fighter Pilot, Technomancer.
- **Advantages:** Military Rank, Faction Gear, Robot Companion.

## 4. Setting Overview
The galaxy is divided between the totalitarian Terran Empire, the mysterious and ancient Protogen, and the bio-mechanical hive mind of the Swarm. Smaller factions and pirate clans fight for survival in the lawless outer systems.

## 5. Proposed Book Structure
1.  Introduction: The Grimdark Future
2.  Character Creation: Soldiers of the Frontier
3.  New Kits & Advantages
4.  Mechanics of War: Squads, Vehicles & Scale
5.  The Galaxy: Factions & Worlds
6.  Equipment & Starships
7.  Running a War Campaign
8.  Threats: Bestiary of Aliens & War Machines
